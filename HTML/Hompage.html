Our Vision

To create a rich, collaborative culture that unites
kura (schools), pouako (teachers), ākonga
(students) and whānau to work collectively on
common goals for the benefit of all.

 ‘Ehara taku toa i te toa takitahi, engari he toa
takitini’ – success is not the work of an
individual, but the work of many.
